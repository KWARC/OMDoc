conclusion

The examples shows, that even though the OMDoc format is rather verbose, the structure of proofs is equivalent to other proof representations. In particular, the explicit management of proof structure by premise links allows to encode the proof as a directed acyclic graph rather than a tree. Note that the restriction to ND calculus is arbitrary for this example, the method slots could contain arbitrary tactics.
